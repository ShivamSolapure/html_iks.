Indian Army

Wars & Operations

1947-48 Indo-Pak War (Kashmir)

Infantry combat, early artillery (Howitzers), rifles (Lee Enfield)

1962 Sino-Indian War

Mountain warfare, rifles (SLR), limited artillery

1965 Indo-Pak War

AMX-13, Centurion tanks, 25-pounder artillery, FN FAL rifles

1971 Indo-Pak War (Bangladesh Liberation)

Vijay tanks, artillery, INSAS rifles (early prototypes), helicopter insertions

1999 Kargil War (Operation Vijay)

Bofors FH-77B, sniper rifles, mortars, Mi-17 air support

Counter-terror operations (Ongoing)

Close-quarter battle gear, night vision, Tavor rifles, bulletproof gear
